In Europe, 80% of the mid- to large size shops are equipped with a video surveillance system. Yet the effectiveness of such systems to detect a shoplifting action is low as the monitors can not be watched continuously. Even in well equipped control centres, a trained operator will have to watch more than 10 screens at a time, making it difficult to detect a shoplifting action which last only a few seconds. Our system detects in real time suspicious actions and generates alerts with a replay of the detected action. A higher detection rate and a lower shrinkage as a result.
