STEP 1

Install the streaming connector in 10 minutes to enable existing camera systems to stream to the cloud.

Footage in the cloud is analyzed real-time by Dektiv with state of the art machine learning algorithms.

Receive alerts

When Dektiv detects shoplifting behavior, a notification is sent immediately to the on-site responsible.

Behind the scenes of Dektiv

Dektiv uses 3D representations to detect shoplifting, focusing on the movements of customers. This eliminates any bias against race, age or gender.
